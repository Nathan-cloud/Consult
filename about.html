Our Vision is to be the most passionately referred consulting firm in Africa. And Our mission is to provide the highest quality service to our clients by combining highly skilled team members with our proven strategies.

The regulatory bodies for the firm and staff are the Institute of Certified Public Accountants of Rwanda (ICPAR) and Association des Taxes Advisors au Rwanda (ATAR) which issue practicing licenses every year. As a medium sized firm we offer reasonable and cost effective services. The size of the firm enables us to respond to client needs with expediency and efficiency. The staff of the firm has absolute commitment to their service. We are dedicated to the development of accounting and auditing standards among fellow Rwandans through the advancement of professional track record.

Our approach to all clients’ services is founded upon understanding of the business needs and problems within each organization. We will only render services, which we believe to be beneficial to our clients. Our familiarity with business cultural, political and legal environment in Rwanda enables us to respond to the client’s needs with practical and realistic solutions.

This is our story

Consultancy and Services Company Ltd is a firm of Certified Public Accountants and other key professionals. Our company is among the medium size indigenous consultancy firms in Rwanda. The firm was incorporated in 2010 under the company Act no. 07/2009 of 27/04/2009 relating to companies in Rwanda. From the staff profile it can be noted that the firm has a team of experienced staff who are conversant with accounting, auditing, taxation, consultancy, training services. The firm also offers other services relating to the core services, this include company receivership and liquidation services and book keeping services. The ability to retain staff and provide team stability comes from the firm significant focus on its staff. We separate ourselves from our rivals by: Timeliness of communications Consistent and experienced staff Availability to clients as a specialized resource Professionalism with understanding Proactive approach in addressing complex issues early in the engagement Offering customized solutions to our customers to enhance quality.
